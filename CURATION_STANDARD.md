# Curation Standard
This repository is curated for trusted producers and collaborators.

## Core Principles
- Credibility over volume
- Official sources over mirrors
- Practical value over novelty
- Clear licensing over ambiguity

## Accepted Sources
- Official vendor/developer websites
- Maintained public repositories
- Reputable educational and technical references
- Legitimate sample/sound libraries with clear terms

## Excluded Sources
- Re-upload dumps with unclear ownership
- Suspicious redirect chains and ad-heavy gateways
- Piracy-adjacent distribution channels
- Pages with unverifiable authorship and no trust signals

## Entry Quality
Each entry should be:
- concise
- category-appropriate
- verifiable in one click
- maintained or historically stable

## Maintenance Policy
- Broken or stale links may be removed without notice
- Repeated low-quality submissions can be declined
- Standards are applied equally to all contributors
