# Q&A FAQ
## What is this repository?
A curated, public-facing quality hub for trusted producer resources.

## Is this only for free resources?
No. Free and paid resources are both accepted if they are credible and useful.

## Do you allow mirror/re-upload links?
No. Submit official source URLs whenever possible.

## Can I submit my own project?
Yes, if it meets the curation standard and has clear trust signals.

## How often are links reviewed?
On contribution cycles and periodic maintenance passes.
