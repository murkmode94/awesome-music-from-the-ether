# Code of Conduct
## Our Commitment
We are committed to a respectful, professional, and inclusive collaboration environment.

## Expected Behavior
- Be constructive and technical
- Respect differing workflows and creative backgrounds
- Focus feedback on content quality and source credibility
- Assume good intent while keeping standards high

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Spam, trolling, or deceptive submissions
- Posting intentionally misleading or unsafe resources

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate this code and may restrict repeat offenders.

## Scope
This policy applies to repository interactions, including issues, pull requests, and discussion threads.
